About The Conference

The proposed international conference represents an important crossroad of business intelligence, AI/ML, data science, and artificial intelligence, strategically positioned to address the evolving landscape of technological advancements. In the dynamic context of today's rapidly changing world, this conference aspires to be a nexus where industry professionals and academics meet to explore, discuss, and propel the frontiers of knowledge in these critical domains.

One distinguishing feature of this conference lies in its commitment to promoting interdisciplinary dialogue. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds, including academia and industry, we aim to catalyse a holistic understanding of the challenges and opportunities posed by these cutting-edge technologies. Another distinctive aspect is the emphasis on applied intelligence, as reflected in the applications for healthcare and medical imaging informatics, cyber security, biometrics, forensics, precision agriculture, risk management, and satellite imaging.

This practical orientation highlights the conference's commitment to translating theoretical advancements into tangible solutions with societal impact. Collaborating with Springer for the publication of conference proceedings will further solidifies the conference's commitment to ensuring the longevity and widespread accessibility of the knowledge disseminated. We will attract papers through a strategic invitation and promotion processes.

The preconference activities like workshops and winter schools in association with partner universities in Mexico (Tamaulipas Autonomous University) and South Korea (Soongsil University, Seoul) will provide a global outreach. Transparent and rigorous review process will ensure fairness and academic excellence.

The Reviewing

We will implement a single-blind peer-review process, assigning a minimum of three reviewers from the Program Committee to each paper submission. Evaluation criteria for the conference include assessing the novelty of the proposal, the presence of up-to-date references, the excellence of scientific writing style, and the proposal's significant scientific and social impact.
