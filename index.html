IBCD 2024

The upcoming international conference is a pivotal convergence point for business intelligence, AI/ML, data science, and artificial intelligence, strategically positioned to navigate technological advancements. It serves as a hub where industry professionals and academics explore and discuss these domains' evolving landscape. Emphasizing interdisciplinary dialogue, the conference aims to foster a holistic understanding of challenges and opportunities. Notably, it focuses on applied intelligence in healthcare, cybersecurity, forensics, and precision agriculture, translating theoretical advancements into real-world solutions. Partnering with Springer for proceedings publication ensures knowledge longevity, and collaborations with universities in Mexico and South Korea provide global outreach. A transparent and rigorous review process guarantees fairness and academic excellence in paper selection.
