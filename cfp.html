IBCD 2024

In the dynamic context of today's rapidly changing world, this conference aspires to be a nexus where industry professionals and academics meet to explore, discuss, and propel the frontiers of knowledge in these critical domains. One distinguishing feature of this conference lies in its commitment to promoting interdisciplinary dialogue. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds, including academia and industry, we aim to catalyse a holistic understanding of the challenges and opportunities posed by these cutting-edge technologies. Another distinctive aspect is the emphasis on applied intelligence, as reflected in the applications for healthcare and medical imaging informatics, cyber security, biometrics, forensics, precision agriculture, risk management, and satellite imaging. This practical orientation highlights the conference's commitment to translating theoretical advancements into tangible solutions with societal impact.

Topics of interest include, but not limited to:

  • Intelligent Agriculture
  • Climate Risk Management
  • Data-Driven Sustainable Finance
  • Energy Savings Supported by ML
  • Sustainable Smart Cities
  • AI's Carbon Footprint
  • Intelligently Control the HVAC-Energy Balance
  • AI and Social Good
  • Healthcare Efficiency
  • Social and Environmental AI Impact on Urbanization
  • Sustainable Digital Twins
  • Trustworthy AI in Face Recognition
  • Trustworthy AI in Health
  • Trustworthy AI in Autonomous Driving
  • Trustworthy AI in NLP
  • Explainable AI Applied in Sustainable Development Goals
