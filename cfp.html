IBCD 2024

In the dynamic context of today's rapidly changing world, this conference aspires to be a nexus where industry professionals and academics meet to explore, discuss, and propel the frontiers of knowledge in these critical domains. One distinguishing feature of this conference lies in its commitment to promoting interdisciplinary dialogue. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds, including academia and industry, we aim to catalyse a holistic understanding of the challenges and opportunities posed by these cutting-edge technologies. Another distinctive aspect is the emphasis on applied intelligence, as reflected in the applications for healthcare and medical imaging informatics, cyber security, biometrics, forensics, precision agriculture, risk management, and satellite imaging. This practical orientation highlights the conference's commitment to translating theoretical advancements into tangible solutions with societal impact.

Topics of interest include, but not limited to:

  • AI and Explainable AI (XAI) for Business Decision Making
  • Computational Mathematics for AI and Machine Learning
  • Data-driven Approaches to Cybersecurity and Threat Detection
  • Privacy-preserving Data Analytics in Business Environments
  • Data Analytics and Data Analysis for Cyber Threat Intelligence and Counter Intelligence
  • Cryptographic Techniques for Secure Data Analytics
  • Aligning Business Analysis for Stakeholders
  • Data analytics to Detect and Mitigate Malware Threats
  • Prediction Analytics for Disease Prevention
  • Data Analytics for Logistics and Transportation
  • Big Data Analytics for Business Intelligence
  • Federated Learning and Privacy-enhancing Technologies
  • Data Driven Business Continuity Planning in the Face of Cyber Threats
  • Supply Chain Resilience through Data-driven Security Measures
  • Marketing Analytics and Consumer Privacy Protection
  • Sports Analytics for Business Operations, and Recruitment
  • Privacy-aware IoT Analytics for Business Insights
  • Ethical AI for Business Intelligence
  • Cryptographic Techniques for Data Analytics
  • Data Analytics for Financial Intelligence and Decision Making
  • Data Analytics for Nature-Inspired Cyber Security


    • Submissions Guidelines

  • Access to the Submission Site
    • URL: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IBCD2024
    • If you have an existing account with the Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit (CMT), please enter your credentials to log in. If you do not have an account, please click on the “Register” button to create one. Your login details and password will be sent to you via email.
  • Initiate a Submission
    • Upon logging in, you will be directed to the Author Console, where all your submissions will be displayed. To create a submission, click on “Create New Submission” at the top left corner of your screen and select the intended track of submission. Please note that after the submission deadline, you will no longer be able to create a new submission.
  • Fill in the Submission Form
    • The submission form requires you to provide the title of the paper, an abstract, full authorship (including co-authors’ information) and other details, which can be changed before the submission deadline. Please ensure that all required fields are completed or your submission will not be saved. Once successfully submitted, your paper will appear in your author console.
  • Upload or Update Your Full Paper:
    • You are required to upload your full paper in MS Word or PDF file format by either dragging it to the box under the “Files” section or by clicking on the “Upload from Computer” button. After the upload is complete, your filename, file size, and upload date and time will appear on the left above the box. You can also update your file with a new version by clicking the “x” next to the file name to delete it, and then upload the new file. Once you have made any necessary changes, click on the “Save Changes” button at the bottom of the submission form.
