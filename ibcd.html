The International Workshop on “BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE, COMPUTATIONAL MATHEMATICS, AND DATA ANALYTICS” (IBCD 2024)

Objective

The iBCD 2024 international conference will serve as a conjunction for business intelligence, AI/ML, data science, and artificial intelligence, strategically positioned to navigate evolving technologies. It will provide a platform for academic and industry professionals to explore critical domains in unison. The conference aims to promote holistic understanding of challenges and opportunities presented by the rise of cutting-edge technologies. It focuses on applied intelligence in healthcare, cybersecurity, forensics, and precision agriculture, translating theoretical advancements into real-world solutions. This conference highlights the commitment to translating theoretical advancements into tangible solutions with societal impact. Partnering with Springer for proceedings publication ensures knowledge longevity, and collaborations with universities in Mexico and South Korea provide global outreach. Furthermore, the iBCD 2024 conference fosters interdisciplinary collaboration by inviting experts from diverse fields to exchange insights and foster innovation. Through interactive workshops and panel discussions, attendees will have the opportunity to delve deeper into emerging trends and best practices, driving forward the collective understanding of these pivotal technologies.

Call for Papers

The objective of the workshop is to involve all top researchers, promising academic scientists, and research scholars to exchange their knowledges and share research results regarding all state-of-the-art developments in various fields that empower sustainability. It also provides an opportunity for educators, practitioners, and researchers to discuss and present the latest trends, and hands-on challenges faced, along with possible solutions proposed for sustainable development. The primary interest of this workshop on ““BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE, COMPUTATIONAL MATHEMATICS, AND DATA ANALYTICS” is to assemble a set of research papers based on novel techniques showing original, current state-of-art and recent research on the given theme, and how they can be of help in providing solutions to problems faced by society.

Topics of Interests:

We invite the submission of original works that are related, but not limited to:

  • AI and Explainable AI (XAI) for Business Decision Making
  • Computational Mathematics for AI and Machine Learning
  • Data-driven Approaches to Cybersecurity and Threat Detection
  • Privacy-preserving Data Analytics in Business Environments
  • Data Analytics and Data Analysis for Cyber Threat Intelligence and Counter Intelligence
  • Cryptographic Techniques for Secure Data Analytics
  • Aligning Business Analysis for Stakeholders
  • Data analytics to Detect and Mitigate Malware Threats
  • Prediction Analytics for Disease Prevention
  • Data Analytics for Logistics and Transportation
  • Big Data Analytics for Business Intelligence
  • Federated Learning and Privacy-enhancing Technologies
  • Data Driven Business Continuity Planning in the Face of Cyber Threats
  • Supply Chain Resilience through Data-driven Security Measures
  • Marketing Analytics and Consumer Privacy Protection
  • Sports Analytics for Business Operations, and Recruitment
  • Privacy-aware IoT Analytics for Business Insights
  • Ethical AI for Business Intelligence
  • Cryptographic Techniques for Data Analytics
  • Data Analytics for Financial Intelligence and Decision Making
  • Data Analytics for Nature-Inspired Cyber Security
Submission:

The workshop is open for the submission of full-length original and unpublished research papers based on theoretical or experimental contributions that represent scientific results, methodical aspects, concepts, and approaches. All submissions must be in PDF format well documented in English language and formatted according to CEUR-WS CEURART style. Templates are available for LaTeX and DOCX/ODT, for single column layout.
