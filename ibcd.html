The International Workshop on “BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE, COMPUTATIONAL MATHEMATICS, AND DATA ANALYTICS” (IBCD 2024)

Objective

The proposed international conference represents an important crossroad of business intelligence, AI/ML, data science, and artificial intelligence, strategically positioned to address the evolving landscape of technological advancements. In the dynamic context of today's rapidly changing world, this conference aspires to be a nexus where industry professionals and academics meet to explore, discuss, and propel the frontiers of knowledge in these critical domains. One distinguishing feature of this conference lies in its commitment to promoting interdisciplinary dialogue. By bringing together experts from diverse backgrounds, including academia and industry, we aim to catalyse a holistic understanding of the challenges and opportunities posed by these cutting-edge technologies. Another distinctive aspect is the emphasis on applied intelligence, as reflected in the applications for healthcare and medical imaging informatics, cyber security, biometrics, forensics, precision agriculture, risk management, and satellite imaging. This practical orientation highlights the conference's commitment to translating theoretical advancements into tangible solutions with societal impact. Collaborating with Springer for the publication of conference proceedings will further solidifies the conference's commitment to ensuring the longevity and widespread accessibility of the knowledge disseminated. We will attract papers through a strategic invitation and promotion processes. The preconference activities like workshops and winter schools in association with partner universities in Mexico (Tamaulipas Autonomous University) and South Korea (Soongsil University, Seoul) will provide a global outreach. Transparent and rigorous review process will ensure fairness and academic excellence.

Call for Papers

The objective of the workshop is to involve all top researchers, promising academic scientists, and research scholars to exchange their knowledges and share research results regarding all state-of-the-art developments in various fields that empower sustainability. It also provides an opportunity for educators, practitioners, and researchers to discuss and present the latest trends, and hands-on challenges faced, along with possible solutions proposed for sustainable development. The primary interest of this workshop on ““BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE, COMPUTATIONAL MATHEMATICS, AND DATA ANALYTICS” is to assemble a set of research papers based on novel techniques showing original, current state-of-art and recent research on the given theme, and how they can be of help in providing solutions to problems faced by society.

Topics of Interests:

We invite the submission of original works that are related, but not limited to:

  • AI & Machine Learning for Sustainable Development Goals
  • Deep Learning for Sustainable Development
  • AI for Disaster Management & Sustainability
  • Architecture & Applications for Data Acquisition and Processing
  • Privacy, Security, and Social Implications on Acquiring Sustainability
  • Ontologies & Semantic Reasoning for Sustainable Development
  • Sustainable Development in Healthcare, Business, and Tourism
  • Efficient, Sustainable Technology for Smart Cities and Futures
  • Sustainable Development & E-Governance
Submission:

The workshop is open for the submission of full-length original and unpublished research papers based on theoretical or experimental contributions that represent scientific results, methodical aspects, concepts, and approaches. All submissions must be in PDF format well documented in English language and formatted according to CEUR-WS CEURART style. Templates are available for LaTeX and DOCX/ODT, for single column layout.
